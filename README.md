# 🍽️ Zomato Restaurant Analysis  

An end-to-end exploratory data analysis project on 51,717 Zomato Bangalore restaurants to uncover customer preferences, pricing trends, and key factors influencing restaurant success. The project also includes an interactive Power BI dashboard for business decision-making.

## 📌 Problem Statement  
Analyze restaurant data to identify patterns in customer ratings, pricing, cuisines, and location trends to help businesses improve performance and customer satisfaction.

---

## 📊 Key Insights  
- Analyzed 51,717 restaurants across Bangalore  
- Identified top-performing locations with highest ratings and demand  
- Found that higher cost does not always guarantee better ratings  
- Popular cuisines vary significantly by location  
- Online ordering availability impacts restaurant reach and customer engagement  

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations  
- Focus on high-demand cuisines in specific locations  
- Optimize pricing strategies based on competitor trends  
- Improve service quality in low-rated regions  
- Enable online ordering to increase visibility and sales
